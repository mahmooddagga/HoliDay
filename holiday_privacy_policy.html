Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Abudaqa-Greencode ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the HoliDay application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: The App may request access to your device's approximate location to automatically detect your country and display relevant public holidays. This data is not stored on our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device information such as device type, operating system version, and app usage statistics for analytics and improvement purposes.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data including device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To detect your country and show relevant public holidays automatically.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To analyze app usage through aggregated, anonymous analytics.

3. Location Permission

The App requests location permission solely to determine your country for displaying local public holidays. We do not track your precise location, store it, or share it with any third party. You can deny this permission at any time from your device settings — the App will still work by letting you select your country manually.

4. Third-Party Services

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With advertising partners (Google AdMob) as described above.
  • When required by law or to comply with a legal obligation.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data collected by third-party services (such as AdMob) is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

  • The right to access information we hold about you.

  • The right to revoke location permission at any time.
